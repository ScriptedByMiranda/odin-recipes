Banana Ice Cream

Description

This homemade banana ice cream is proof that simple can be really good. All you need is frozen bananas and a little skim milk — blend it up and you've got something that actually tastes like ice cream in under a minute.

Ingredients

  • 2 peeled and chopped bananas, frozen
  • ½ cup skim milk

Steps

  1. Combine frozen bananas and 1/4 cup skim milk in a blender; blend for 30 seconds.
  2. Add remaining 1/4 cup milk and blend on high speed until smooth, about 30 seconds more.
